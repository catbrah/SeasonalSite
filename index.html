What is a seasonal job?

Seasonal jobs are typically short-term jobs in the hospitality and tourism industries.

Seasonal jobs are primarily entry-level positions with little to no experience needed.

These jobs typically are located in National Parks, amusement parks, resorts, cruise lines, camps, ranches, and other interesting places.
